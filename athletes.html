7 of the Most Educated Olympians
Who Competed at the Paris Olympic Games

Malaika Mihambo

Malaika Mihambo is a German long jumper who won gold at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics and silver at Paris 2024. She completed her undergraduate studies in political science at the University of Mannheim and is currently pursuing a Master’s in environmental sciences at the University of Hagen.

Shelly-Ann Fraser-Pryce

Shelly-Ann Fraser-Pryce, the Jamaican sprint queen, has won multiple Olympic medals. She holds a BS in Child and Adolescent Development from the University of Technology and has received honorary doctorates for her contributions to society.

Gabby Thomas

Gabby Thomas, an American sprinter, won three gold medals at Paris 2024. She completed her Master’s in epidemiology at the University of Texas Health Science Center and holds a degree in neurobiology from Harvard University.

Hugues Fabrice Zango

Hugues Fabrice Zango, from Burkina Faso, is a world indoor record holder in the triple jump and won a bronze at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics. He earned a Master’s degree and a PhD in Electrical Engineering from the University of Artois.

Aitana Bonmati

Aitana Bonmati, a top football player, helped Spain win the 2023 FIFA Women’s World Cup and earned the Ballon d'Or. She has a degree in physical activity and sports science and is pursuing a Master’s in sports management at the Johan Cruyff Institute.

Hans Henken

Hans Henken, representing Team USA in sailing, secured a bronze medal in the Men’s Skiff event. He holds a BS and MS in Aeronautical and Astronautical Engineering from Stanford University, focusing on rocket propulsion.

Rachael Gunn

Rachael Gunn, Australia's representative in breakdancing, holds a BA in Contemporary Music and a PhD in Cultural Studies from Macquarie University. Her PhD thesis focused on gender in breakdancing.
